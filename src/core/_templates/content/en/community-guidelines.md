**1. Respect and Safety**

a) Treat all interactions on the platform with respect and professionalism.

b) Do not attempt to use the Service to plan, coordinate, or promote any real-world harmful, illegal, or violent activity.

c) Do not use the Service to harass, threaten, or intimidate any person.

**2. Prohibited Content and Behavior**

The following is strictly prohibited on the Service:

a) **Illegal content:** Content that violates local, national, or international law, including content related to illegal drugs, weapons trafficking, terrorism, or fraud.

b) **Hate speech:** Content that promotes hatred, violence, or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

c) **Violence and self-harm:** Content that glorifies, encourages, or provides instructions for violence, self-harm, or suicide.

d) **Non-consensual content:** Content that uses the likeness of real persons without their consent, or depicts real persons in a harmful or misleading manner.

e) **Impersonation:** Attempting to impersonate real individuals, public figures, or other users with deceptive intent.

f) **Platform abuse:** Attempting to reverse-engineer, exploit, or circumvent platform safety measures, rate limits, or content moderation systems.

g) **Spam and manipulation:** Automated bulk actions, fake accounts, or any activity designed to manipulate platform features or other users.

a) You are solely responsible for the content you create, upload, or share through the Service.

b) You must comply with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction when using the Service.

c) Do not share sensitive personal information (financial details, addresses, passwords) on the platform.

d) Keep your account secure. Do not share your login credentials with others.

**4. User-Generated Content**

Content created by users represents their own views and opinions. %SITE_NAME% does not endorse or take responsibility for user-generated content.

Do not rely on user-generated content as factual information or professional advice.

**5. Age Requirement**

The Service is intended for users who are of the age of majority in their jurisdiction. If you are underage, you may not use the Service.

Violations of these Community Guidelines may result in:

a) Warning or temporary restriction of features;
b) Content removal;
c) Temporary or permanent account suspension;
d) Reporting to law enforcement authorities (for serious violations).

The severity of enforcement depends on the nature and frequency of the violation. Serious violations (threats of violence, illegal content) result in immediate account termination and reporting to authorities.
