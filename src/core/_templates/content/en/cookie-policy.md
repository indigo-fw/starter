**1. What are cookies?**

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide analytics information, and enable certain features. We also use localStorage for certain functional purposes, which is described below.

**2. Types of cookies we use**

**2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies**

These cookies are essential for the operation of the Service. Without these cookies, the Service cannot function properly. These cookies do not require your consent.

| Cookie                               | Purpose                                | Duration |
| ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------- | -------- |
| `__Secure-better-auth.session_token` | Maintains your authenticated session   | 1 year   |
| Cookie consent cookie                | Stores your cookie consent preferences | 1 year   |

**2.2 Analytics Cookies**

These cookies allow us to understand how visitors interact with the Service by collecting and reporting information. We use these insights to improve the Service. These cookies are loaded via Google Tag Manager (GTM).

| Cookie   | Purpose                                                        | Duration |
| -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| `_ga`    | Google Analytics — distinguishes unique users                  | 2 years  |
| `_gid`   | Google Analytics — distinguishes unique users within a session | 24 hours |
| `_gat`   | Google Analytics — throttles request rate                      | 1 minute |
| `_gac_*` | Google Analytics — stores campaign information                 | 90 days  |

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie             | Purpose                                                                                                     | Duration |

**3. Third-party cookies**

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. Third parties that may set cookies include:

- **Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager** — analytics and conversion tracking
- **Cookie consent provider** — cookie consent management
- **Payment processors** — functional cookies required during payment processing

Each third-party service has its own privacy and cookie policy. We encourage you to review them.

**4. Cookie consent management**

When you first visit the Service, a cookie consent banner will be presented. You can choose to accept or reject non-essential cookie categories.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie preferences widget that remains accessible on the Service.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies

Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent the Service from functioning properly.

**5. How to withdraw consent**

You can withdraw your consent for non-essential cookies at any time by:

- Clicking the cookie preferences widget on the Service
- Clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the Service (a new consent banner will appear)
- Adjusting your cookie preferences in your browser settings
